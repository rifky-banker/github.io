About Me

Professional Biography

I am Rifky, an accomplished Senior Banking Professional with extensive experience spanning across prestigious international banking institutions in Sri Lanka. Throughout my 19+ year career, I have cultivated a deep expertise in Islamic Finance, SME Banking, and Supply Chain Finance.

My approach to banking is rooted in a strategic blend of traditional financial acumen and innovative product development. This duality allows me to structure solutions that not only meet complex client requirements but also adhere strictly to regulatory frameworks and inclusive banking principles.

Currently pursuing a Doctorate with a research focus on Green Banking, and holding an MBA from Cardiff Metropolitan University, I am committed to advancing sustainable financial ecosystems. My academic pursuits directly inform my professional practice, particularly in driving digital transformation strategies within the commercial banking sector.

Professional Philosophy

"To empower economic growth by Engineering innovative, transparent, and inclusive financial solutions that bridge the gap between institutional capabilities and client aspirations."
